Strange Encounter (Mikipedia)

Figure: Alleged extraterrestrial and unidentified flying object.

This photograph captures one of the strangest moments of my life.

At first, it seemed like an ordinary night: silence, stars, nothing out of the ordinary. But then he appeared—gray, smooth, as if made in a toy factory, and he looked at me as if he were assessing: "Is this person worth trading for batteries?"

The flying saucer nearby seemed to be simply parking, as if this weren't space, but an ordinary supermarket parking lot. Moreover, it looked as if the alien had emerged briefly to check if anyone had scratched the side.
