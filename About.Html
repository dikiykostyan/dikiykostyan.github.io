Welcome to Mikipedia — Wikipedia’s smaller, sillier cousin.

Mikipedia is like Wikipedia, but:
• with less information than a tea bag after the third brewing;
• faster, because there’s almost nothing to load;
• made by one person, but hey — at least he tries.

Here we collect **almost-facts**, questionable knowledge, and everything that didn’t make it into the real Wikipedia. If you find a mistake — don’t worry, it has friends.
